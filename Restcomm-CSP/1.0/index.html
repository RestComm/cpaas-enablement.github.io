Getting started with Restcomm

Restcomm CPaaS allows Service providers to add real-time communication features (Voice and SMS) to their own applications with no need to build complex telecom infrastructure and interfaces. You can get started by registering and setting up your Restcomm organisation, defining your styling, bringing your own carrier and then start onboarding enterprise customers.

Bring Your Own Carrier (BYOC)

Bring Your Own Carrier (BYOC) feature lets you connect to the platform and allows your enterprise customers to use your network by default for PSTN traffic. BYOC enables you to layer the value of a modern CPaaS on top of your existing PSTN investment.

Go to market with your brand - Whitelabel

Restcomm’s white labeling feature is specially designed for Service providers and aims to give you an opportunity to differentiate from the competition, by offering a unique Unified Communications (UC) offer, while reducing costs and time to market. You no longer need to spend time and effort on product development. With Restcomm, you can rebrand the platform and make it look like it is your own, using a self-service interface.

Message Exchange

Thanks to Restcomm’s Message Exchange, you) can enable your enterprise customers to send and receive SMS using their Unified Communication (UC) client. Enterprise customers are able to send two-way SMS or send a message to multiple destinations.

Accounts

Easily onboard your enterprise customers by allowing them to register for an account under your own CPaaS.
