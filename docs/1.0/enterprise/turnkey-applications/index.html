Turnkey Applications

Explore a wide range of CPaaS use cases and industry best turnkey applications.

Smart 2FA

Secure online transactions, account sign-ins, mobile apps, websites, and more by providing smart 2FA with one-time passwords to your users.

Call Queuing

Call Queuing allows callers to be placed in a Queue, hearing background music or announcement (text to speech) while waiting to get connected to called party.

Auto Attendant

Auto Attendant (AA) is intended for business customers who want to provide an automated means of directing incoming calls to the appropriate person within the organization, typically to offload this task from a receptionist and also offer a simple menu system.

Number Masking

Protect user identity and keep their phone numbers private by connecting them anonymously.

Cisco Webex

Cisco Webex helps Business Customers to send and receive SMS using Webex Teams.

Task Router

Task Router is a skill based task routing to an agent. A task can be generated from any channel.

Campaign Manager

Campaign Manager allows to send the same SMS to hundreds of thousands of phone numbers within minutes.
