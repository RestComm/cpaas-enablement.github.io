Phone Number Masking

In this tutorial, you will learn how to create a number masking application for a taxi service with CPaaS Number Masking. The application will connect the customer and the taxi driver through masked phone numbers, while both of their identities stay protected. Follow the steps below to get started.

Prerequisites

In order to set up your application, you will need to have an Enterprise account created in your CPaaS solution under your Communications Provider and enabled access to the Number Masking turnkey application.

Registering the Masked Numbers

You will have to register two phone numbers that will later be used as masked numbers in order to protect the identity of the customer and the taxi driver.

  • Log into the CPaaS Dashboard.

  • Navigate to the tab Numbers → Register Number → Provider Number.

  • Select the country and area codes.

  • Set the Capabilities to Voice.

  • Register two numbers as shown below.

You need to have a minimum of two phone numbers registered in order for Number Masking to work.

Creating Your Number Masking Application

  • Navigate to the Number Making Console (https://yourdomain.com/numbermaskingconsole/).

  • Click on the Numbers tab.

  • The phone numbers registered in the previous step will be available in the list of numbers, similar to the screenshot below.

  • Click on the Applications tab.

  • Click on the Add Application button.

  • Specify the name of the application and hit Done.

The newly created application will appear in the list of Applications.

Adding Masked Numbers to Your Application

  • Click on the name of the newly created application.

  • Go to Mask Numbers → Add Mask Number.

  • Select and add the two numbers from the list.

Adding Context to Your Application

  • On the same screen, navigate to Context → Add Context.

  • Fill out the Context form as shown below.

  • Click Done.

Adding Participants to the Context

  • Click on the Participants link in the newly created context.

  • Click on the Add Participants button.

  • You will need to add two participants (Customer and Taxi driver) to the Context.

  • Fill out the Add Participants form with the customer details.

The phone number field corresponds to the customer’s actual phone number. The Mask number field is the customer’s masked number registered in the previous steps.

  • Click Done

Repeat the steps above on a behalf of the taxi driver.

Your Number Masking Application in Action

You are all set! Every time the customer calls a taxi from their phone number to the driver’s masked number (+1111 to +13053902732), the taxi driver will appear to receive a call from the following caller ID: +13053878126l. With Number Masking, both parties can effectively communicate without exposing personal phone numbers.
